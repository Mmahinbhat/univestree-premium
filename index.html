About Univestree

Empowering Students,
Institutions & Educational Growth

We are a collective of passionate professionals, education counselors, innovators, and strategists dedicated to transforming the future of education through guidance, mentorship, technology, and institutional excellence.

✨ Founded in July 2025
A Vision Started by Abdul Basit Bedar

For over a decade, Abdul Basit Bedar has dedicated himself to guiding students through one of the most defining journeys of life — education.

Having counselled thousands of students, he believes that education is not merely about degrees, but about discovering purpose, direction, and one’s true potential.

As the visionary Founder & CEO, his mission is to ensure that no student walks through confusion and uncertainty alone.

Through sincere mentorship and proper educational guidance, he strives to help students choose a path that leads not only to success — but to meaning, clarity, and lifelong impact.
